What is Smithies in CS?
Smithies in CS is a peer-to-peer student interest group for Computer Science. We regularly host events to help educate the community on tech (i.e. hackathons, learnathons), provide mentorship and support group for students who want to join the tech industry, and provide tech resources on conferences, fellowships, and hackathons for underrepresented communities in tech. We welcome students of all majors and minors to join our club.
You can officially join our group by signing up through the Social Network Organization for Smithies in CS. We also encourage you to join our Slack and Facebook groups as they have active communities providing networking and job opportuniities as well as tech resources. If you are interested in Data Science, please visit the Smithies in Stastiscal and Data Science Group.
This website is to provide a central place in which students can find tech resources, guidance on major planning, as well as information on upcoming Smithies in CS events.
